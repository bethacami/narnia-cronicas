SYNOPSIS

THE CHRONICLES OF NARNIA

In the midst of World War II, four English siblings are evacuated to the countryside to escape the terrible bombings that are ravaging London. What they don't imagine is that their refuge hides an unimaginable secret. One day, the curious Lucy Pevensie, the youngest of the siblings, discovers an old wardrobe that not only holds clothes, but is also the door to an enchanted kingdom: Narnia. When she tells her siblings, no one believes her at first, but soon, Peter, Edmund Pevensie and Susan are also drawn into the magical world, where time runs differently and fantastic creatures inhabit every corner. Together, they will be involved in an epic battle between good and evil, led by the majestic lion Aslan, and must face the tyranny of the sinister Queen Jadis, who has condemned Narnia to eternal winter. An adventure full of magic, danger and discovery awaits them on the other side.

REASONS TO WATCH THE CHRONICLES OF NARNIA

MAGICAL AND VISUALLY STUNNING WORLD

The Chronicles of Narnia transports you to the fascinating world of Narnia, filled with magical creatures, winter landscapes and epic settings. The visual effects and production design bring this fantastic universe to life in spectacular fashion.

DEEP THEMES OF REDEMPTION AND SACRIFICE

Through characters like Aslan and Edmund Pevensie, the film explores universal themes such as forgiveness, redemption and sacrifice. This adds an emotional and moral layer that enriches the experience, making it more than just a fantasy adventure.

LOVELY CHARACTERS AND PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

The Pevensie siblings, especially Lucy and Edmund, show emotional and spiritual growth. This evolution adds depth to their characters, creating a connection that resonates with viewers of all ages.
