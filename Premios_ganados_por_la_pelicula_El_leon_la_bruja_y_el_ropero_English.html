The Narnia victories that dazzled the world!



The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe not only won hearts, it also swept up all the major awards! From its stunning Oscar for Best Makeup to the BAFTA awards for its magical visual effects, the Narnia film was celebrated for its technical excellence. It also received the Saturn Award for Best Fantasy Film and took home the Teen Choice Award as the favorite among young audiences.
